I am 3rd year PhD student at Laboratoire de Physique des Plasmas, in Ecole polytechnique, France. I work under the supervision of [Özgür D. Gürcan](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=IR3T5hoAAAAJ&hl=fr) and Nicolas Fedorczak. My thesis subject is [Transport Modelling for Fusion Devices Using Reduced Models for Turbulence](https://theses.fr/s367107).

I am interested in turbulent transport in magnetised plasmas of tokamaks, and the self-organisation of turbulence into large-scale coherent structures, such as zonal flows.

I mainly use the Hasegawa-Wakatani system as the minimal, non-trivial plasma turbulence model, in order to understand zonal flows formation and build reduced model which account for their interaction with turbulence.

I also developped [P-FLARE](https://github.com/piergui/P-FLARE) a pseudo-spectral code using the penalisation method in order to solve flux-driven reduced fluid systems for plasma turbulence.
